Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 9, 2024

Summary: Gentle Wellness does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Introduction

Gentle Wellness ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the extension handles your information.

This extension is developed by Venkataramana Balaji and operates with complete transparency regarding data practices.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Gentle Wellness does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Browsing history or website URLs you visit
  • Search queries or search history
  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location data or IP addresses
  • Device information or hardware identifiers
  • Analytics, cookies, or tracking data
  • Any other personal or identifiable information

Data Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only using Chrome's sync storage:

  • Reminder Settings: Your preferences for eye care, posture, hydration, movement, and mindfulness reminders (enabled/disabled status and time intervals)
  • Blue Light Filter Settings: Your filter preferences including mode (Light/Standard/Strong), intensity level, and warmth level
  • Dark Mode Settings: Whether dark mode protection is enabled or disabled

Important: All settings are stored using Chrome's built-in sync storage. If you're signed into Chrome with sync enabled, your settings may sync across your devices through Google's servers. This is handled entirely by Chrome, not by our extension.

How the Extension Works

Reminder System

The extension uses Chrome's Alarms API to schedule wellness reminders at intervals you set. These reminders:

  • Run locally on your device
  • Do not send any data externally
  • Display notifications only on your device
  • Can be enabled, disabled, or customized at any time

Blue Light Filter & Dark Mode

The blue light filter and dark mode features work by:

  • Injecting visual overlays on web pages you visit
  • Applying CSS styling and visual filters directly in your browser
  • Not reading, modifying, or collecting any website content
  • Not accessing forms, passwords, or personal information on pages
  • Working entirely offline without external connections

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions. Here's exactly why each one is needed:

  • notifications: Required to display wellness reminder alerts at your chosen intervals (eye care, posture, hydration, movement, mindfulness)
  • alarms: Required to schedule recurring reminders. We use Chrome's Alarms API instead of JavaScript timers because it persists across browser restarts and doesn't consume resources when idle
  • storage: Required to save your preferences locally, including reminder settings, filter intensity, warmth levels, and enabled/disabled states. All data stays on your device

Content Script Access (all websites): The extension injects a content script on all websites to apply the blue light filter and dark mode overlays. This script ONLY creates visual filter layers and does not read, modify, collect, or transmit any page content, user data, or browsing information. The broad access is necessary because the filter must work on any website you visit.

What We Do NOT Access

Even though the extension runs on all websites, it does NOT:

  • Read website content or text
  • Access form inputs or user-entered data
  • Capture passwords or login credentials
  • Monitor browsing history or URLs
  • Track user behavior or analytics
  • Modify website functionality or content
  • Inject advertisements or tracking scripts
  • Communicate with external servers

Third-Party Services

Gentle Wellness does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No external APIs or servers
  • No social media integrations
  • No tracking pixels or beacons
  • No cloud services or databases

The extension operates completely independently and offline.

Data Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, we have nothing to share. We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Share data with third parties
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Use your data for advertising or profiling
  • Provide data to law enforcement (we have no data to provide)

Children's Privacy

Gentle Wellness does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The extension is safe for users of all ages.

User Control

You have complete control over the extension and your data:

  • All settings can be changed at any time through the extension popup
  • Individual reminders can be enabled or disabled independently
  • Filters can be adjusted or turned off instantly
  • You can reset all settings to defaults with one click
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all stored settings permanently
  • No data persists after uninstallation

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted:

  • There is no risk of data breaches from our servers (we have no servers)
  • Your settings are as secure as your Chrome browser
  • No passwords or credentials are ever stored by the extension
  • The extension uses Chrome's built-in security features
  • All code runs locally in your browser sandbox

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Transparency

The extension code is designed with transparency in mind. All data handling occurs locally within your browser, and no external connections are made. We believe in being completely transparent about what our extension does and doesn't do.

Your Rights

Under various privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you typically have rights regarding your personal data. Since Gentle Wellness does not collect any personal data, these rights don't directly apply. However, you always have the right to:

  • View all stored settings (visible in the extension popup)
  • Modify any setting at any time
  • Delete all settings (use the "Reset to Defaults" button)
  • Control what the extension does (enable/disable any feature)
  • Uninstall the extension at any time

Compliance

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
